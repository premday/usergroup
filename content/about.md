Premday is made up of organisations that operate on-premises infrastructure at scale and share a commitment
to openness and collaboration. It is born from an observation: on-premises practioners have the same technical
issues, needs, and same difficulty to find the right contacts to get their problem solved.

To fix that, we created the Premday initiative:
- an annual conference to share best practices, experiences, ideas and feedbacks to both other infrastructure owners and vendors.
- a user group working together on a common goal: help each other, foster collaboration with vendors, express collective needs, and influence the future of this industry. 

## Attending the conference

The annual Premday conference is the fastest way to meet the community in person.

See [premday.org](premday.org) for more information.
